Our Story

Zentro Homes was founded in 2020 by a group of young, passionate investors who shared a common frustration: finding quality investment properties in Kenya's rapidly growing real estate market was unnecessarily difficult and time-consuming.

What began as informal meetups to share property leads and investment strategies quickly evolved into a structured approach to solving this challenge for themselves and others.

The founding team—consisting of architects, finance professionals, and real estate enthusiasts—combined their diverse expertise to create a comprehensive property sourcing and management service. They recognized that many potential investors, both local and international, were missing out on lucrative opportunities in Kenya's property market due to lack of reliable information, trustworthy partners, and efficient processes.

Zentro Homes was built on the principle that property investment should be accessible, transparent, and rewarding. The team developed a rigorous vetting process for properties, ensuring that each listing meets strict criteria for location, build quality, rental potential, and long-term appreciation.

Our properties consistently deliver rental yields between 7-12%, significantly outperforming many other investment options available in the market.

Today, Zentro Homes has grown into a trusted partner for investors seeking quality properties in Kenya's most promising locations. Our portfolio includes carefully selected apartments, villas, and commercial properties that offer excellent returns while minimizing the typical headaches associated with property investment.

We take pride in our personalized approach, working closely with each investor to understand their goals and match them with properties that align with their investment strategy. Our comprehensive management services ensure that your investment continues to perform optimally with minimal effort on your part.

The Zentro Homes team remains committed to our founding vision: making property investment in Kenya straightforward, reliable, and profitable for investors from all backgrounds.
